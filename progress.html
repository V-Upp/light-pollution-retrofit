July 12, 2026

Milestone: Cobra-Head Retrofit Hood v1.0 Assembly Finalized

Completed the primary solid-body CAD configuration for our first formal design review. Core mechanical additions include:

  • Modeled 13mm side-shielding wings for glare reduction.
  • Subtracted four 25x75mm ventilation slots to lower calculated wind resistance.
  • Designed the top photocell collar to avoid fixture sensor blocking.
  • Isolated the 610x178x3mm Amber Filter Panel as a standalone, cleanable component.

Next Step: Export the STEP assembly into structural wind-loading simulators and check compliance with regional TxDOT utility thresholds.
