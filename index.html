Falsification & Abandonment Criteria

To maintain objective scientific integrity, we have established clear thresholds that would require a complete redesign or outright cancellation of this concept:

  • Illuminance Drop: If simulations show roadway target-area illumination decreases below safety margins, we will abandon the geometry.
  • Thermal Trapping: If thermal modeling indicates heat buildup shortens the existing LED lifespan, we will discard the current enclosure design.
  • Wind Shear: If the added surface area creates wind loading that exceeds standard TxDOT utility pole limits, the concept will be deemed unviable.
