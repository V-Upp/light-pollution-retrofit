Primary Open Technical Questions

Optical Performance:

What is the exact percentage shift in roadway target-area illuminance when adding peripheral shielding profiles?

Thermal Integrity:

Does enclosing the upper casting flange cause local heat entrapment that speeds up driver or diode degradation?

Falsification & Core Abandonment Thresholds

To verify this inquiry adheres to objective engineering methods rather than commercial confirmation bias, development will immediately halt or pivot if any of the following metrics are met:

[01] Photometric Drop

If ray-tracing models verify that downward pavement illuminance dips below standard municipal traffic safety margins.

[02] Thermal Spikes

If steady-state thermal analysis yields operating junction temperatures that cause premature LED output degradation.

[03] Wind Shear Bounds

If the calculated wind drag coefficient of the shield exceeds structural tolerances allowed for standard utility mast arms.
