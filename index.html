Optimizing Our Night Skies

Investigating low-cost, scalable retrofits to mitigate urban light pollution from existing cobra-head streetlights.

About Us

We are two high school students in North Texas interested in engineering and environmental sustainability. This project began after noticing unnecessary light spill from roadway lighting in our community. Our goal is to learn from engineers while investigating whether an inexpensive retrofit could reduce unwanted light without replacing existing infrastructure.

Open Engineering Questions

We are actively investigating the following technical challenges:

  • How much would roadway illuminance change?
  • How much glare reduction is achievable?
  • Does the hood significantly affect cooling?
  • Is the added projected area acceptable under wind loading?
  • Would maintenance crews consider the retrofit practical?

Disclaimer: This is an early-stage engineering concept intended for discussion and technical review. It has not been validated through structural, thermal, or photometric testing.
